`cf orgs`  
> Lists all organizations.
> 
> `cf spaces`  
> Lists all spaces  
> in the current organization.
> 
> `cf target`  
> Shows the current target.
> 
> `cf target -o <org>`  
> Switches the organization.
> 
> `cf target -s <space>`  
> Switches the space.
> 
> `cf target -o <org> -s <space>`  
> Switches both.
> 
> `cf apps`  
> Lists all apps.
> 
> `cf services`  
> Lists all services.
> 
> `cf help`  
> Shows the help page.
